iDrug
Multi-scenario DTI Prediction and Personalized Drug Recommendation Support Platform. iDrug is an upgraded platform built for multi-scenario drug-target interaction prediction and personalized drug recommendation support. It combines scenario-specific DTI workflows, structured drug and protein knowledge, and patient-aware reranking to provide lightweight, explainable assistance for drug discovery and medication decision support.

Platform Architecture

iDrug adopts a three-layer architecture:

  • Base Layer 1: Multi-scenario DTI Prediction Supports drug repositioning, target screening, drug evaluation, and affinity prediction through task-specific models.
  • Base Layer 2: Knowledge Query Provides structured drug, protein, and complex information with support for 3D visualization.
  • Core Layer: Personalized Recommendation Re-ranks candidate drugs with patient-specific features and outputs interpretable results.
